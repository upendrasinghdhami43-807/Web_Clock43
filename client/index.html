Alarm Overview

StClock helps you create dependable daily reminders with clear sound alerts and quick stop controls. Use this section to understand the main alarm flow before setting your first schedule.

  • Choose your alarm time and preferred ringtone.
  • Select ring duration that matches your routine.
  • Keep notification volume ready for reliable wake-up alerts.

How To Use

  1. Open Settings from the top-right controls.
  2. Set Alarm Time, Audio Cue, and Ring Duration.
  3. Click OK to save your settings.
  4. Use Test Alarm Audio to verify sound output.
  5. Leave the app open so alarms can trigger on time.
